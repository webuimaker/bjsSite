ALL ROADS LEAD TO HOME

Award winning two-man home delivery teams, trained in excellence and trusted by leading brands. With an enviable track record of more than a decade the BJS family offer a range of flexible services to keep you and your customers happy.

DELIVERING AN EXTRA HELPING HAND FOR GROWING BUSINESSES

BJS Extra is all the experience of BJS Home Delivery with an extra helping hand for up and coming companies. We give you a boost by welcoming you to the delivery team big league, without penalising you for low volumes. We can support you with our IT systems and processes and help you to grow.

WITH YOU AT EVERY TURN

BJS Haulage is the UK’s leading national haulage company and can help you to navigate the twists and turns of your day-to-day nationwide transport needs with a service-led approach. We have big trucks, big ambition and huge potential to help you be good at what you do, by being great at what we do.

SPECIALISTS IN BRICK AND BLOCK HAULAGE

Construct IT provides a safe and reliable service for market-leading construction product manufacturers. An impressive modern fleet delivers outstanding service and specialist haulage for the construction industry, backed by a first-class IT system and DVS compliance as standard.

What makes BJS different

We provide the very best customer service at every stage of the delivery process, putting customers at the heart of everything we do – so that your products, reputation, customers and peace of mind really are safe in our hands.
